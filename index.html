About Me

I am a dedicated professional with expertise in AutoCAD, GIS, Remote Sensing, and Data Management. Holding a Master's degree in Geography and Environment and currently pursuing a Doctoral Program in Environmental Science & Technology, I am passionate about contributing to the fields of Environmental Science, Natural Resource Management, and Disaster Management.

With experience as a Research Consultant at CEGIS, GIS/RS Assistant, and Field Surveyor, I excel in visual interpretation, data analysis, and geodatabase editing. I also served as General Secretary of the Green Environment Movement, promoting awareness and sustainable practices.

Currently, I focus on sustainable construction materials that mitigate resource depletion, reduce pollution, and lower costs. I seek opportunities to contribute to impactful projects globally.
