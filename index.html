About Me

I am a dedicated professional with expertise in AutoCAD, GIS, Remote Sensing, and Data Management. Holding a Master's degree in Geography and Environment and currently pursuing a Doctoral Program in Environmental Science & Technology, I am passionate about contributing to the fields of Environmental Science, Natural Resource Management, and Disaster Management.

With a proven track record as a Research Consultant at the Geographic Information System Division in a renowned organization Center for Environmental and Geographic Information Services (CEGIS), I excel in visual interpretation, data analysis, and geodatabase editing. Additionally, my experience as a GIS/RS Assistant and a Field Surveyor further enhances my skill set. Committed to environmental conservation, I have also served as General Secretary of the Green Environment Movement, where I led teams in promoting awareness and sustainable practices.

Currently, I am deeply interested in building and construction and I am particularly enthusiastic about creating sustainable construction materials that can help mitigate natural resources depletion, pollution and reduce construction costs. I am eager to leverage my potential, adaptability, and expertise to contribute to the success of a reputable organization that shares my passion for making a positive impact in the world.
